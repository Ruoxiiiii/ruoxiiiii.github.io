At the Threshold explores human curiosity and the intention to move closer while choosing to remain at a respectful distance. All images are photographed from outside buildings, often using fences as framing elements that mark a clear physical boundary.

Vital Drift consists of images of birds moving across open sky, where atmosphere and spatial expanse take precedence over the subject itself. The series explores a strength that is present but not loud, vitality expressed through subtle movement rather than spectacle.

Where the Sky Meets gathers compositions in which sky meets horizon, forming a line that extends beyond the frame. It invites viewers to pause, breathe, and immerse themselves in the honesty of the open sky.

Where Attention Rests centers on where the eye settles naturally, spaces that feel warm, grounded, and safe. Through quiet compositions and gentle contrasts of light and shade, the images create a sense of comfort where no additional effort is required.

Held Over Time focuses on trees observed from different angles across days and seasons. The images reveal the energy, persistence, and resilience held within them as time accumulates and seasons turn and return.
