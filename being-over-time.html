Being, Over Time

[cite_start]

Being, Over Time explores life, aging, emotions, and relationships as ongoing cycles that move from physical existence to emotional and spiritual experience[cite: 1]. [cite_start]Beginning with the body and everyday emotional states, the work follows how these conditions change through interaction with others[cite: 2].

[cite_start]

Using simplified systems and scientific measures such as heart rate, arousal, and valence, the project reflects both change and unchange across growth, emotion, and repetition[cite: 5].
